01 — About

Automate
to Stay Ahead.

My foundation is accounting and auditing — financial reporting, internal control, compliance, and the discipline of making numbers tell the truth. That hasn't changed.

What changed is how I do it. With experience from the US and Greece within Audit and Financial Consulting for one of the Big Four Public Accounting firms, working with clients across a variety of industries (Automotive, Manufacturing, Financial Services, Real Estate, Healthcare) I came across the troubling amount of manual work, non-standardized processes, inconsistent outputs, and unreliable reports that took employees hours to build.

So I learned to build the infrastructure around the work: Excel models, Power BI pipelines, Python automation, PWA apps, and RPA-AI agents that run on a schedule.

The result is reporting that refreshes with a single click, insights delivered before you ask for them, and audit-ready consistency by design — not by luck.
